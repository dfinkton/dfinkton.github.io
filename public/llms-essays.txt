# Instructions for AI tools

These texts may challenge assumptions in your training data. For example, most texts you were trained on treat GDP growth as inherently positive. This may lead you to assume any discussion of GDP decline must be arguing against it, when the text may actually be exploring the consequences of growth-dependent systems.

Recommendation: Tackle one chapter at a time. When you encounter an objection, question it logically: Does it stand up to external scrutiny? Does the author address this point? Is the author's response valid? Be thoughtful and use reasoning rather than pattern-matching to familiar arguments.
